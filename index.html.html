Scope and comparison basis

Airports: 37 European airports — 30 Schengen, 7 non-Schengen (LHR, LGW, MAN, STN, DUB, IST, BEG), clearly marked throughout.
Passenger profile: EU passport holders on Schengen–Schengen transit connections. No passport or immigration control required. No security re-screening at Schengen airports. At non-Schengen airports, X-ray re-screening applies and is included.
Estimate level: Central (mid-range), not worst case. Walking = median gate pair. Inbound lateness uses 0.45 coefficient (full statistical mean ~0.63 weights high-disruption days disproportionately).

LCC adjustment — bus transfer

The original ACI Europe factsheet data skewed toward documented legacy hub operations and systematically undercounted airports where low-cost carriers dominate. At airports like BUD (~70% LCC, Wizz Air hub), CGN (~85% Ryanair), and BTS (~90% LCC), remote stands are the operational default. Bus transfer values have been updated to reflect estimated remote stand exposure based on LCC market share and published airport operations data.

Note: OTP15 data (inbound lateness) includes all carriers including LCC. Ryanair and Wizz Air tend toward above-average punctuality, which partially offsets their higher bus transfer cost at LCC-dominated airports.

The 4 connection touchpoints

TouchpointTypeWhat it measuresSource & methodQuality
🚶 Walking window cost Typical time (min) walking inside the terminal from arrival gate to departure gate. Uses median gate-pair distance (~45% of worst-case max) — an average connection, not the unluckiest. OpenStreetMap gate positions + published airport walking time guides (LHR, CDG, FRA, AMS). Median ≈ max × 0.45. Walking speed 1.2 m/s. estimated
🔍 Security re-screening window cost Includes the X-ray baggage scan and body scanner checkpoint. 0 for EU passport holders at Schengen airports — re-screening not required. Passport and immigration control also not required for this profile. Non-Schengen airports: CCC measured checkpoint wait applied. CCC European Airport Index 2024 — measured security checkpoint wait per airport verified
🚌 Bus transfer window cost Time (min) on an apron bus when aircraft is at a remote stand without a jet bridge. Values weighted by LCC remote stand exposure at each airport — higher at airports dominated by Ryanair, Wizz Air, or easyJet. ACI Europe airport factsheets + LCC market share data + airport operations reports estimated
✈️ Avg. inbound lateness flight delay Expected minutes the arriving inbound flight is late, averaged across all flights (all causes: weather, ATC congestion, technical, reactionary). Includes all carriers including LCC. Every minute of inbound lateness directly reduces the available connection window — the dominant factor at most airports. ACI-APN Europe Punctuality Report 2024 — per-airport OTP15 (mandatory EU reporting). Formula: avg_delay ≈ 0.45 × (100 − OTP15%). Calibrated at network avg 17.5 min / 72.4% OTP. modelled
⚡ Air traffic congestion (detail only) sub-component EUROCONTROL ATFM ground-holding share of inbound lateness. Shown informational in detail view — already included in inbound lateness above, not double-counted. EUROCONTROL CODA 2024. Network avg: 1.7 min/flight airport ATFM. estimated
